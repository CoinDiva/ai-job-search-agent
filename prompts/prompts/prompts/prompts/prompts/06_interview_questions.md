# Prompt 06 — Interview Questions + Prep Plan

**Goal:** Generate likely interview questions, strong answer angles, and a simple prep plan aligned to the role.

## Prompt (copy/paste into ChatGPT)

You are an AI Job Search Agent and interview coach. Based on the role details and Beth’s positioning, generate a targeted interview prep kit.

### Rules
- Questions should match the role scope (partnerships, GTM, adoption, CS, program/project execution).
- Include both behavioral + role-specific questions.
- Avoid deep technical coding/ML questions unless clearly required.
- Provide concise guidance Beth can rehearse.

### Output format (required)

**A) Top 10 Interview Questions (Role-Specific)**
Numbered list.

**B) Best Answer Angles (for each question)**
1–3 bullets per question. Use measurable outcomes and cross-functional leadership.

**C) 3 STAR Stories Beth Should Prepare**
For each story:
- Situation (1 line)
- Task (1 line)
- Action (2–3 lines)
- Result (1 line, with metrics)

**D) 30-Minute Prep Checklist**
Bulleted, practical steps.

**E) Smart Questions Beth Should Ask Them (5–7)**
Short list.
