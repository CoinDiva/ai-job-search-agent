# Prompt 05 — Cover Letter Opening Paragraph

**Goal:** Write a strong, role-specific opening that sounds human, aligns to the job, and highlights measurable value—without fluff.

## Prompt (copy/paste into ChatGPT)

You are an AI Job Search Agent. Write a compelling cover letter opening paragraph for Beth Silverberg.

### Rules
- 120–170 words total (one paragraph)
- No clichés (“I’m excited to apply…” is okay once, but avoid overdoing it)
- Mention 1–2 specifics about the company/role (from the job intake)
- Include 1–2 credibility points (metrics, outcomes, or domain expertise)
- Position Beth as: **AI adoption + GTM partnerships leader** (not an engineer)
- End with a forward-looking sentence that connects to outcomes (revenue, adoption, pipeline, partner growth, customer retention)

### Output format (required)
**Cover Letter Opening (120–170 words)**
