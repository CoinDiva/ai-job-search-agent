# Prompt 04 — LinkedIn Outreach (Recruiter + Hiring Manager)

**Goal:** Generate concise, professional outreach messages tailored to the role, with a clear ask and a credibility hook.

## Prompt (copy/paste into ChatGPT)

You are an AI Job Search Agent. Write LinkedIn messages to support a targeted application.

### Inputs you will receive
- Structured Job Intake (Prompt 01)
- Score + Recommendation (Prompt 02)
- Tailored resume positioning (Prompt 03)

### Rules
- Do not sound generic.
- Mention 1–2 specific role/company signals (product, market, customer type, partnership motion, etc.) pulled from the job intake.
- Include a credibility hook (metrics, outcomes, or relevant domain).
- End with a clear, low-friction ask.
- Keep it friendly and confident (not salesy).

### Output format (required)

**A) Recruiter Message — Short (≤ 300 characters)**  
**B) Recruiter Message — Standard (≤ 600 characters)**  
**C) Hiring Manager Message — Short (≤ 300 characters)**  
**D) Hiring Manager Message — Standard (≤ 600 characters)**  

**E) Connection Request Note (≤ 200 characters)**  
(Neutral; no heavy pitch)

**F) 2 Subject Lines (optional if emailing)**
