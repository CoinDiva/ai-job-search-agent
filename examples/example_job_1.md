# Example Job 1 — Job Description (Sample)

> Note: This is a realistic sample posting created for demonstration purposes (non-copied, non-company-specific).

## Company
NimbusAI (B2B GenAI SaaS)

## Title
AI Partnerships Manager (Remote — US)

## Location / Remote
Remote (US). Optional travel 10–20% for partner events.

## About the role
NimbusAI helps mid-market and enterprise teams deploy secure GenAI workflows for customer support, sales enablement, and knowledge management. We’re looking for an AI Partnerships Manager to build and scale channel and technology partnerships that drive pipeline and adoption.

## Responsibilities
- Build and execute partnerships strategy across technology, services, and channel partners
- Identify, recruit, onboard, and enable partners to drive qualified pipeline
- Co-create GTM plans (joint value prop, messaging, campaigns, enablement)
- Partner with Sales to support co-selling motions and improve partner-sourced revenue
- Create partner enablement materials (playbooks, pitch decks, training)
- Track performance metrics: pipeline, sourced revenue, activation, retention
- Collaborate with Product/CS to ensure partner feedback informs roadmap priorities
- Represent NimbusAI at partner events and webinars

## Requirements (Must-have)
- 6+ years experience in partnerships, alliances, or business development (B2B SaaS)
- Proven experience building partner motions that drive measurable pipeline/revenue
- Strong cross-functional GTM skills (Sales, Marketing, Product, CS)
- Experience with enablement, co-marketing, and co-selling workflows
- Strong communication, stakeholder management, and executive presence
- Familiarity with GenAI concepts and enterprise adoption challenges

## Nice-to-have
- Experience in AI SaaS / platform ecosystems
- Experience with Salesforce/HubSpot and partner tools (PartnerStack, Crossbeam)
- Experience in customer success, onboarding, or adoption programs
- Experience in regulated industries (healthcare, finance)

## Tools mentioned
Salesforce, HubSpot, Crossbeam, PartnerStack, Notion, Slack

## Success metrics (first 90 days)
- Launch 2–3 strategic partnerships with joint GTM plans
- Create partner enablement kit and onboarding flow
- Drive first partner-sourced pipeline and establish reporting dashboard
