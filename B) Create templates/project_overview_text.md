## Summary (≤200 characters)
Built a ChatGPT-based AI job search agent that scores remote AI roles and generates ATS keywords, tailored resume bullets, and outreach messages.

---

## Solution (≤500 characters)
The agent turns an unstructured job search into a repeatable workflow: it extracts requirements from job posts, scores fit using a weighted rubric with penalties for overly technical roles, and generates application-ready outputs (ATS keywords, tailored bullets, recruiter/hiring manager outreach, cover letter opener, and interview prep). This improves focus on high-fit roles and increases the quality and consistency of applications.

---

## Approach (≤500 characters)
1) Defined target roles, constraints (remote-first), and success metrics. 2) Built a 100-point scoring rubric with technical-fit penalties. 3) Created prompt templates for job intake, scoring, resume tailoring, outreach, and interview prep. 4) Validated the workflow using sample job posts and saved example inputs/outputs plus a tracking template to support repeatable daily use.

---

## Longer “Describe your key findings, recommendations, and process” (profile visible)
Key finding: job searches are inefficient when roles are evaluated inconsistently and application materials aren’t tailored. Recommendation: use a structured AI-assisted workflow to (1) normalize job requirements, (2) score fit with a transparent rubric, and (3) generate consistent, ATS-aligned application assets. Process: I defined target AI business roles, created a weighted scoring model with penalties for engineering-heavy roles, built reusable prompt templates for intake/scoring/tailoring, and added examples and a tracker to make the workflow repeatable.
